IIT Consultancy - Purpose & Importance
IIT Consultancy bridges the gap between elite IIT graduates and top employers through a modern, secure job matching platform. Candidates access verified job listings and personalized dashboards, while employers efficiently discover and hire premium talent.

Why it matters:

Talent Access: Direct pipeline to IIT's world-class engineering graduates

Efficiency: Real-time applications, role-based workflows, automated matching

Transparency: Secure JWT auth, status tracking from apply→interview→hire

Modern UX: Glassmorphism design ensures engaging, mobile-first experience

Impact: Democratizes access to IIT talent for SMEs, streamlines recruitment for enterprises, empowers candidates with data-driven career tools. Production-ready platform solving India's premium tech hiring challenge.
